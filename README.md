Global E-Commerce Sales Dashboard
Advanced Business Intelligence using Power BI
Project Overview

The Global E-Commerce Sales Dashboard is a business intelligence solution developed using Microsoft Power BI. It provides a comprehensive analysis of global sales data, enabling users to extract meaningful insights and support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard converts raw transactional data into structured visual representations across markets, product categories, customers, and regions.

Objectives

Analyze global sales performance across multiple regions
Identify high-performing products and customers
Evaluate category-wise sales and profitability
Enable dynamic and interactive data exploration
Support strategic business decision-making

Key Metrics and Insights

Total Sales: ₹4.30M
Total Profit: ₹502K+
Total Quantity Sold: 61K
Average Shipping Time: 4 days

Business Insights

Technology is the most profitable category
Asia Pacific leads in overall sales performance
Profit is concentrated among a limited number of products
A small segment of customers contributes significantly to revenue

Dashboard Features

Interactive filters for Year, Quarter, and Market
Sales and Profit analysis by category
Regional sales visualization using tree maps
Top 5 and Bottom 5 products based on profit
Customer-wise profit distribution
Structured and user-friendly dashboard layout
